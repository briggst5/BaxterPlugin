### Variants

The Split button comes in two variants: both are useful when you need to show multiple options but have limited screen space available to display them. However, they enable two different interactions and have slightly different behaviors. Check the Usage Guidelines tab to understand which variant better suits your needs.

### Interaction States







### Option Menu

The Split Button is designed to trigger a dropdown menu that offers multiple options for selection. This component features both a closed and an open state. The open state borrows theDropdown Menu component in its “Button” variant.







### Leading Icon

Use the Icon Left boolean property to show or hide the leading icon.
