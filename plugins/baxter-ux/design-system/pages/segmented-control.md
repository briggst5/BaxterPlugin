## Segmented Control



### Anatomy

A Segmented Control is composed of at least two Items  inside a track. Its width is variable and should be adjusted depending on the grid, although each item's size should ensure a minimum tappable area in compliance with Design for all guidelines.







### Size Specifications

This component usually has a fixed width, with each segment occupying an equal amount of space inside. Segment Items widths are defined by the width of the container track (minus padding) divided by the number of options. See the Usage Guidelines for more guidance on Layout.







### Number of Segments

A Segmented Control is made of at least two Segment Items, one of which is marked as the current selection. The component can support up to six segments. Designers are free to detach the component if more than six segments are needed, although that’s not recommended.
