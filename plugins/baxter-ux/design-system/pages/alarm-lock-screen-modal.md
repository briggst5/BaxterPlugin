### Severity variants

Each severity is associated with a specific color and icon, ensuring priority is visually communicated and creating a consistent and universal user experience of interpreting alarms.



Example of Very low severity Lock Screen Modal on Med Surg Bed.





### Screen variants

Based on screen size and ratio, content layout adapts to make the most out of the available space.



Examples of Medium severity Alarm Lock Screen Modal on Spot Monitor and Infusion Pump.





### Style Variants

Content drives structure: these variants are helpful to differentiate what takes center stage in different types of alarms, minimizing the user's cognitive load to interpret the alarming condition and understand next steps.



Example of High severity alarm, Default Style on Infusion Pump.

Example of High severity alarm, Vitals Style on Spot Monitor.

Example of High severity alarm, Icon only Style on Med Surg Bed.
