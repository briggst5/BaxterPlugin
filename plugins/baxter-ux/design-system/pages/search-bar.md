### Style variants

Style variants enable the search bar to adapt to its background, ensuring optimal contrast and a visually appealing outcome. The Search Bar comes in two main variants: one designed for neutral backgrounds and another for colored backgrounds, typically featuring the brand color.









### State variants









### Filled

The isFilled boolean property determines whether the filter has an assigned input value or if the label serves as a descriptive placeholder.
In an expanded search bar with content, an interactive "x" icon appears: it allows users to clear the field, resetting its state to isFilled: false.









### Collapsed

You can configure the search bar to be collapsed by setting isCollapsed to true. Typically, tapping or clicking on a collapsed search bar will trigger it to expand.
