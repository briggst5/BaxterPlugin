### Style Variants

Utilize the Variant boolean property to modify the shape of this component, allowing you to switch seamlessly between linear and circular designs.







### Linear

The linear progress bar is a horizontal element that typically fills from left to right. However, when utilized for countdowns, the animation direction can be reversed to indicate time running out. This feature enhances user understanding of the remaining time visually.



#### Label

Use the Label property to add or hide this element. To enhance visibility, position the label in close proximity to the progress indicator. This strategic placement ensures that users can easily identify the status.

The label can indicate either a generic percentage or a precise number – of steps (e.g. 2/5) or other valuable formats, such as time remaining – to give users an additional indication of the progress. Even without a label, a progress bar can serve as a valuable visual cue, helping users gauge the duration of a process when an exact percentage isn't available.

### Circular

The circular progress bar serves as a visual representation of progress over time, allowing for both incremental growth and countdown animations. It can be effectively utilized in various applications to enhance user experience by providing clear feedback on task completion or visualising a countdown.

The circle can either be a generic visual cue or divided by steps, giving a more precise visual indication of the state of completion and informing users of their precise advancement in a process like in the Step Progress Circle component.
