# Work Item Ownership


| Work Item | Owned By | Focus  |
|--|--|--|
| Epic  | Business Owner, Architect, Product Manager | Large strategic initiative  |
| Feature | Product Manager & Product Owner| Program-level capability |
| Story | Product Owner | Iteration-level deliverable |
| Task | Agile Team | Workable item |



## Epics
[Epics](https://dev.azure.com/FLC-NPD/FutureState/_wiki/wikis/Platform.wiki/10/Work-Item-Hierarchy#epics) are larger initiatives and often represent significant amounts of work.

Epics are owned by: _Business Owner_, _Architect_, or _Product Manager_

The responsibilities for an Epic owner are:
- Define the Epic.  SAFe calls this the [Epic Hypothesis](https://framework.scaledagile.com/epic)
- Collaborate with stakeholders, owners, and architects
- Drive the Epic through completion
- Ensure proper decomposition into _Features_
- Accept completion of Epics once the Epic is complete and demonstrated

## Features
[Features](https://dev.azure.com/FLC-NPD/FutureState/_wiki/wikis/Platform.wiki/10/Work-Item-Hierarchy?anchor=features) are services or capabilities that deliver value within a PI and scoped to an Agile Release Train (ART).

Features are owned by: _Product Manager_, _Architect_, _Product Owner_

The responsibilities for a Feature owner are:
- Prioritize features in the Program Backlog
- Define the Description and Acceptance Criteria
- Collaborate with architects and teams to define necessary enablers (expressed as predecessors in ADO)
- Accept completion of Features once the Features are complete and demonstrated

## User Stories
[User Stories](https://dev.azure.com/FLC-NPD/FutureState/_wiki/wikis/Platform.wiki/10/Work-Item-Hierarchy?anchor=user-stories) are workable items sized for a single iteration.

Stories are owned by: _Product Owner_

The responsibilities for a Story owner are:
- Move story from a promise to talk about it later to a workable item
- Prioritize in Team Backlog
- Collaborate with Agile Team to define the story
- Define and refine user stories during Backlog refinement
- Ensure stories align with the feature objectives
- Accept stories once acceptance criteria and Definition of Done are met
- Demonstrate story completion during PI reviews and demos

*Note: The Agile Team can propose a User Story at any time, but it requires review, acceptance, and prioritization with the Product Owner. This ensures scope is controlled and iteration commitments are maintained relative to defined business objectives.*

## Tasks
[Tasks](https://dev.azure.com/FLC-NPD/FutureState/_wiki/wikis/Platform.wiki/10/Work-Item-Hierarchy?anchor=tasks) are workable items that are completed within a single iteration.

Tasks are owned by: Agile Team

The responsibilities for a Task owner are:
- Review and accept the task assignment
- Drive the task to completion consistent with the Definition of Done
- Demonstrate task completion during Iteration Demos
