# Strategy for Managing Development of New Assets

One of the key determinants between success and failure in the development of a common software platform is having a clear governance that:
- ensures proper feature prioritization, 
- that the platform remains focus on delivering incremental value, and 
- prevents accumulation of technical debt.

## Decision Flow
The following decision flow is from the Platform governance model.  A pithy summary of this model is "_Its Platform until it isn't_".  Crucially, there is a clear decision path that is feature based and focuses on the cost-value trade-offs to deciding reusability.
