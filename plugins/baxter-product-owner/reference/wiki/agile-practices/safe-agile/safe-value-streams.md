# Agile Practices/SAFe Agile/SAFe Value Streams

SAFe value streams can be defined as follows:
1.  **Compliance Value Stream**:
    *   Focuses on ensuring that products and services meet regulatory and legal requirements.
    *   Involves activities like audits, reporting, and risk management.
2.  **Core Product Value Stream**:
    *   Centers on delivering the main products or services of the organization.
    *   Includes all steps from concept to delivery that create value for customers.
3.  **Culture Value Stream**:
    *   Aims to foster a positive organizational culture that supports Agile practices.
    *   Involves activities that promote collaboration, learning, and continuous improvement.
4.  **Customer Experience Value Stream**:
    *   Focuses on enhancing the overall experience of customers with the organization.
    *   Includes feedback loops, customer support, and engagement activities.
5.  **Enabling Value Stream**:
    *   Supports other value streams by providing necessary resources and capabilities.
    *   Involves activities like training, tooling, and infrastructure development.
6.  **Innovation Value Stream**:
    *   Encourages the development of new ideas and solutions.
    *   Involves research, prototyping, and testing to bring innovative products to market.
7.  **Support Value Stream**:
    *   Ensures that customers receive ongoing assistance and service after product delivery.
    *   Includes help desk support, maintenance, and troubleshooting activities.
8.  **Sustainability Value Stream**:
    *   Focuses on practices that promote environmental and social responsibility.
    *   Involves activities that reduce waste, improve efficiency, and support sustainable practices.
These value streams help organizations align their efforts to deliver value effectively across different areas.
