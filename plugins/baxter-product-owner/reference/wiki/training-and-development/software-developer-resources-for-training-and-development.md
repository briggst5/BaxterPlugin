## IEC 62304

## GQP 09-05
### Software Architecture Design Specification (SADS)
Software architectural design plays a central role in ensuring that a software system is safe, reliable, and testable.  Software architecture encompasses the set of critical decisions about the organization of a software system in pursuit of those goals.  From a process standpoint, the SADS is often viewed as the bridge between SW requirements and implementation.

For more details on SADS from the perspective of compliance to GQP-09-05, see [Architecture Documentation](https://worksites.baxter.com/:p:/s/FLCPlatform/IQARcZ4RqEhiRYN9F2Nr93WNAW7EyW5ZJDje7xIYCh5T5K0?e=hTtcuJ)
