# Future State

# Overview

In the Scaled Agile Framework (SAFe), the _Future State_ is the envisioned condition or outcome of a solution that an organization aims to achieve.  It represents the desired improvements and capabilities that align with customer and stakeholder needs.  The _Future State_ is the guiding vision for future improvements, is entails creating an action plan to determine how to transition from current state to future state.  

The purpose of this DevOps organization is for us to lead the change to a new way to develop products and deliver value to our stakeholders and customers.

Part of this transformation is technology, but a larger part is adopting the SAFe Agile Framework as the basis for a new way of work.  To support that, we have **extensive documentation in the [Agile Practices](/Agile-Practices)** section of the wiki.  For those seeking more, there is a link to [training available through BaxU](https://dev.azure.com/FLC-NPD/FutureState/_wiki/wikis/Platform.wiki/49/Agile-Training-Available).
